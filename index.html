The AttendX Process

A revolutionary 5-step system that ensures accurate and efficient attendance

1

Activation

Teacher activates the attendance system by pressing a button on their app

2

Signal Broadcast

System broadcasts secure signals throughout the entire classroom

3

Signal Detection

Students' mobile apps automatically receive and detect the classroom signals

4

Identity Verification

Signal detection triggers automatic face capture and matching against pre-registered student data

5

Attendance Confirmed

Successful identity match confirms attendance and updates the teacher's real-time dashboard
